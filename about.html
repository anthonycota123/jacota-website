Our Story

JA Cota Inc. started as a father-son dream to bring honesty and craftsmanship back to construction in Las Vegas. With over 35 years of combined experience, we've framed thousands of homes, built dozens of custom projects, and created a reputation for quality, accountability, and leadership on job sites across Nevada.
